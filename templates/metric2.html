Coefficient of traffic manipulation:

info
The Coefficient of Traffic Manipulation is expressed as follows, where C is the coefficient of traffic manipulation, R is the percentage of retweets, F is the percentage of traffic from the top fifty users, and U is the average number of tweets per user:

C = R/10 + F + U

In our case, we get the following result:

{{coeff}} = {{rt}} /10 + {{fifty}} + {{average_tweets}}
